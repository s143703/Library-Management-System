LMShelf Library Management System




Welcome to LMShelf, the digital library management system designed to make accessing and managing knowledge easier, faster, and more organized.


Our mission is to simplify how students, faculty, and staff interact with the library, providing an intuitive platform to search, borrow, and return books without the delays and limitations of traditional systems.


The LMShelf project was developed as part of an academic initiative to modernize library operations using web technologies. By integrating automation, secure data management, and a user-friendly interface, we aim to enhance the overall experience of both library users and administrators.
